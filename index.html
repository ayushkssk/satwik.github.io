Recent Projects

1. Kidzee

Kidzee is the largest play school in India with 1900+ centres in over 750 cities and has garnered a colossal repute in a decade’s period. They have categorised their programmes into Nuvo, Playgroup, Nursery, and Kindergarten for the sheer convenience of the parents. It’s the most successful pre-school chain in Asia. Every child is endowed with their pedagogy, Interactive iLLUME, that helps children discover their learning style. They also provide their students with publications and other learning blogs. Enrol your little ones here to acquire outstanding education! Where | Kidzee - Saket, GK I, East Patel Nagar & Other Location

2. The Magic Years

If you want your child to have a global exposure right from the start, this school is the place to be at because they have 200+ students from over 15 nations. The school follows the teaching methodology of Montessori. They have an incredible teacher-student ratio of 1:4 which ensures individual attention to every child. The school provides the children with lunch and drinking water. The facility possesses child-friendly furniture, outdoor area and sports equipment. So do check them out!

3. Little Pearls

This fine play school offers a holistic learning approach to children aged between 16 months to 6 years. The classes are fully air-conditioned so that your kiddos don’t become a victim of heat stroke during the gruelling summer months. In addition to schooling, they also offer daycare facilities to children whose parents are working. The best part about this play school is they use the GPS system for safe pick up and drop!

4. The Ardee School

This marvellous play school is spread over half an acre in Lutyen’s Delhi. Their teaching methodology is a concoction of different study models like Montessori, Play Way, and Reggio Emilia models. They have an attractive teacher-student ratio of 2:20. And if you are a pure vegetarian then this school is perf for your kid, as children are given a hearty vegetarian meal. They also have an eco-friendly campus, sandpit and water play zone. So enrol here right away!

5. Mother’s Pride

Your kids will learn tons of new stuff at this play school as they house a ball pool and projector room which facilitate virtual learning, splash pool to beat the summer heat and a computer library which makes the child tech-friendly. They also have a dollhouse which is a virtual world meant to help children in social development. It’s an activity centre where kids are allowed to play independently. The dolls represent themselves and their family and due to this unique blend of fun and learning, children are able to develop socio-emotionally. So what are you waiting for? Apply ASAP!

6. Little Creative Minds Playschool

This is one of the oldest preschools for kids in the city and offers a wide range of features like multiple intelligence building programmes and more. They offer weekly lessons plans and the class worksheets are sent to the parents to track the progress of the child. They also host really fun workshops for the kids such as clay modelling, arts and craft, theatre workshop, and storytelling workshop amongst others. This school is one of the best picks for a brighter future!
